Artificial turf is an excellent low-maintenance alternative in Winnipeg. It looks great year-round and is ideal for high-use areas or shaded lawns where natural grass struggles.

Simple landscaping projects can take a few days, while more complex ones may take 2 to 3 weeks. Timelines are always discussed during our planning phase.

Hardscaping refers to non-living features like patios, stone paths, and walls, while softscaping includes soil, plants, sod, and mulch. We specialize in both.
