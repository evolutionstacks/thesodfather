About Landscape

A landscape is the visible functions of a location of land, its landforms, and just how they integrate with natural or human-made features, often thought about in terms of their aesthetic appeal. A landscape consists of the physical components of geophysically specified landforms such as hills, hills, water bodies such as rivers, lakes, fish ponds and the sea, living components of land cover consisting of aboriginal plant life, human components including different kinds of land use, structures, and frameworks, and temporal elements such as lights and climate condition. Integrating both their physical beginnings and the social overlay of human existence, usually developed over millennia, landscapes show a living synthesis of individuals and location that is essential to local and national identification. The personality of a landscape aids define the self-image of individuals who inhabit it and a sense of place that differentiates one region from other areas. It is the vibrant background to people's lives. Landscape can be as varied as farmland, a landscape park or wilderness. The Planet has a substantial range of landscapes consisting of the icy landscapes of polar areas, hilly landscapes, huge arid desert landscapes, islands, and coastal landscapes, largely forested or wooded landscapes including previous boreal woodlands and tropical jungles and farming landscapes of temperate and exotic areas. Frequently Asked Questions

What landscaping features are low-maintenance but visually appealing?

Features like decorative gravel, native plants, mulch beds, and stone walkways require less maintenance and hold up well in our seasonal climate.

How often should I water new sod?

New sod should be watered daily for the first week, then every other day for the second. After that, you can transition to a regular lawn care schedule depending on rainfall.

Can I hire you just for sod installation?

Absolutely. Sod installation is one of our most popular services. We handle everything from soil prep to final watering instructions.

How can I improve drainage in my yard?

We install custom drainage solutions such as French drains, proper grading, and permeable paver systems to help prevent pooling and foundation damage.
